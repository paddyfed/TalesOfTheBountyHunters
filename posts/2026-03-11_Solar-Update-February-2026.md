I am able to schedule it to charge longer on the days that I might be in Cavan without having to change the normal 2-4 am charging slot. Hopefully with the weather getting better I'll be getting more out of each charge. There's also been a day or two where the car has gotten some power from the solar panels.

Consumption this month is roughly the same as January at 162kWh with 681kWh imported. It is of course a shorter month by a few days. Export was a bit better than last month at 13kWh but down on last year which was 27kWh.
